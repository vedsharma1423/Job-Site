Engineering Jobs

Design and build machines and processes to help mankind in all kinds of tasks.

Electrical Engineering

Create and test electrical equipment such as circuits, motors, generators, and even navigation system. This is a relatively recent field that uses principles of electricity to create devices that can help human beings.

Average base salary: $100,000

Required Knowledge: A degree in electrical/electronics engineering. Good understanding of physics.

Mechanical Engineering

Create devices of any kind from refrigeration, air conditioning to steam and gas turbines. This is a very important field as it helps humsn beings in all types of tasks.

Average base salary: $90,000

Required Knowledge: A degree in Mechanical Engineering and strong knowledge in areas such as physics, chemistry, and fluid mechanics.

Computer Engineering

Use knowledge of computer science and electronic engineering to create computer hardware components such as CPUs, GPUs, main memory, and secondary memory. Also work on software components such as operating systems, networking systems, and others.

Average base salary: $50,000

Required Knowledge: A degree in Computer Engineering and knowledge in how computer hardware and software systems work.
